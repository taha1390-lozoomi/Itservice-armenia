Our Working Process

A structured onboarding, deployment, and technical maintenance cycle for seamless corporate operations.

1. Initial Requirements Consultation

We review your current technical environment, business structure, and team workflows to determine the exact requirements for your custom accounting software setup.

2. Environment Setup & Installation

Our specialists completely install the application on your designated company workstations and local server infrastructure, preparing the platform for configuration.

3. Custom System Configuration

We map your internal financial rules, roles, and organizational structure directly into the software settings to ensure optimal system behavior and workflow alignment.

4. Safe Deployment & Operational Launch

We oversee the live launch phase of the accounting system, executing system diagnostics to eliminate initial errors and guarantee an obstacle-free operational kickoff.

5. Specialized User Training

When required, we deliver comprehensive training sessions for your staff to familiarize them with the software tools, maximizing workspace efficiency from day one.

6. Continuous Maintenance & Remote Support

We offer ongoing monitoring, error fixing, and official software updates via internet-based remote assistance to handle issues immediately without operational delay.

7. Flexible Invoicing & Retainer Management

All services operate under clear terms. Standard monthly retainers are processed via professional corporate invoicing with a clear 10-business-day completion window.
